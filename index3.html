About Us

Built on Grit,
Driven by Vision

One Man Success was founded by Atnafu Getaneh Kassa with a singular purpose — to unlock the mineral wealth of Ethiopia's Assosa region through hard work, honest practice, and deep local knowledge.

With over 2 years of hands-on experience in gold mining, Atnafu has quickly built a reputation for reliability and results across the Benishangul-Gumuz region. What began as a one-man operation is growing into a trusted mining enterprise serving clients and partners throughout the region.
